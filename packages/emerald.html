Truss & Structure

A premium-scale truss structure—among the largest inclusions in the industry—built to deliver dramatic ceiling depth, visual impact, and a truly grand wedding atmosphere.

Plan View
Portrait View

What if the venue requires coverage larger than 1,500 sq. ft.?

The Emerald Package includes 30 ft x 50 ft (1,500 sq. ft.) of truss coverage. Should the venue require additional coverage, extension is available at ₱50 per sq. ft., subject to venue layout and structural assessment.

What if the required truss area is less than 1,500 sq. ft.?

The full truss allocation remains reserved for your event to ensure structural balance, design flexibility, and consistency of the ceiling treatment. Package value remains unchanged.

What if the truss will be provided by another supplier?

If truss will be supplied by a third party, our team will coordinate accordingly. Please note that ceiling styling designs under the Emerald Package are optimized for Migs Styling Production's truss system, and design adjustments may apply depending on compatibility and load capacity.
